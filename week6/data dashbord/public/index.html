Priyanka and Gracy created two small houses each called "Casa Amor" to collect data in each person's enviornment. Priyanka is using a Light Color sensor to collect rgb color values of the light in her apartement. Gracy is using a sound sensor and a temperature and humidity sensor to collect data in her apartment. The data is being sent through a MQTT broker and are displayed in real time below.
